I'm a final-year M.Tech student in Medical Devices at NIPER, Mohali — and I've spent the last two years deliberately sitting at the intersection of two worlds most people keep separate: the lab and the boardroom.

On the other, I'm building dashboards, automating regulatory workflows, and doing the kind of data work that turns a question like "which market should we enter first?" into a defensible answer.

I genuinely believe the next generation of great medical devices will be built by people who understand both the science and the system around it — regulatory pathways, clinical evidence, market access, business impact. That's the kind of professional I'm trying to become.

MedTech Regulatory Pathway Navigator

I built the MedTech Regulatory Pathway Navigator — an interactive decision-support tool that recommends the optimal regulatory pathway (CDSCO, FDA 510(k), or CE Mark) for any medical device based on its risk class, intended use, and target market. It encodes 3 major regulatory frameworks into a single tool, compressing pathway scoping from hours to minutes. Built with Python, Streamlit, Pandas, Plotly, and Excel-based MDR 2017 / FDA / EU MDR rule sets. Regulatory scoping is often the first bottleneck in a device launch — this removes it.

Clinical Trial Landscape Intelligence Dashboard

I built the Clinical Trial Landscape Intelligence Dashboard — a consulting-grade competitive intelligence tool that pulls live data from the ClinicalTrials.gov API across trial phases, geographies, sponsors, and dropout rates for any device or therapy category. It covers 1,000+ trials per query and ships with a 3-slide client-ready PowerPoint summary. Built with Python, Pandas, SQL, Power BI, the ClinicalTrials.gov REST API, and PowerPoint. Due diligence on clinical landscapes shouldn't require a research team — this puts that power in a single dashboard.

ISO 13485 QMS Gap Analysis Automation Tool

I built the ISO 13485 QMS Gap Analysis Automation Tool — a Python-powered tool that accepts a company's QMS documentation checklist and auto-generates a risk-prioritised gap analysis report mapped against every ISO 13485:2016 clause, with actionable CAPA recommendations. It reduces manual gap analysis effort by ~60%, producing output that mirrors real audit deliverables. Built with Python, openpyxl, python-docx, Excel, Lucidchart, and PDF export. QMS gap analysis is a consulting service billed at thousands of dollars per engagement — this automates the bulk of it.
